the scariest place in the universe

is the contemplation of
a last breath as we walk the
tightrope between being
tired of life and tired of living.
and every night, when the
earth breaks my heart and
tosses me aside, leaving me
to desire for nothing,
i wonder whether Cupid
lost his aim first, or his care.
